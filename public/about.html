About

The Google Autocomplete Archive saves daily predictions from Google Autocomplete, using Google Web (the regular Google), Google Images, and Youtube, in 24 different languages.

How it Works

Exactly as if you typed the letters from A to Z, one at a time, on Google, Google Images, and Youtube. The difference is that it is repeated for multiple languages everyday, and it is done by a computer.

What it Shows

Some of the most popular searches on those languages. Things like:

  • French sometimes google "utube" when searching for Youtube.
  • The periodic table is a hit.
  • People really like quotes. Love quotes, life quotes, friendship quotes, inspirational quotes, funny quotes, Valentine's Day quotes...
  • There are remaining Guns N' Roses fans in Spanish-speaking countries and in Brazil.
  • Pictures of horses are incredibly popular in Denmark, Iceland, Norway, and Finland. Check out letter H if you don't believe me.
  • There are people still using Hotmail.
  • Images of diseases are quite popular: yeast infection, measles, haemorrhoids...

If you find something interesting, send me an email!
